Cybersecurity and Information Security

Protecting your information and enhancing cybersecurity.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, and networks from malicious attacks. It involves protecting systems and data from unauthorized access, damage, or theft.

What is Information Security?

Information Security focuses on protecting information in all forms, whether digital or physical. It ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data, safeguarding it from unauthorized access and breaches.
